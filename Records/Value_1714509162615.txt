SENTIENT SPECIES:
Feathered Koboldoids: Eat pretty much anything, including one another. Disturbingly fond of skulls. They have bilateral symmetry. They are violent and believe in the survival of the fittest. They first evolved on Wotan II in 372.
Slim Deep Dwellers: They live in the deep oceans or in deep caves, hiding. Their presence can go unnoticed for a long time. They have bilateral symmetry and a slim shape. They are thrill-seeking and strive to uphold their ideals of personal honour. They developed from Amorphous Deep Dwellers in 230.
Amorphous Red Robots: Purely mechanical lifeforms, artificially created. They have red skin. They have bilateral symmetry and are able to greatly alter their shape. They are reclusive and have a complex system of social rules. They were created by the Second Empire of Six-Legged Humanoids with Five-fold Symmetry on Delut IV as servants in 353.
Scaly Humanoids: Pretty adventurous but otherwise fairly average. They have bilateral symmetry. They are meditative and value personal integrity above all else. They first evolved on Eunir VI in 208.
Grey Catoids with Long Tails: Always curious and often cruel. They have grey skin. They have bilateral symmetry. They use their long tails for balance. They are modest and deeply religious. They first evolved on Hylus V in 293.
Feathered Yellow Ursoids: Always at war with someone, or everyone, or one another. They have yellow feathers. They have bilateral symmetry. They are gregarious and strive to uphold their ideals of personal honour. They first evolved on Wotan II in 246.
