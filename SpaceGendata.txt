SENTIENT SPECIES:
Four-Armed Humanoids with Five-fold Symmetry: Pretty adventurous but otherwise fairly average. They have four arms. They have five-fold symmetry. They are violent and have a deep need to conform. They developed from Four-Armed Humanoids in 56.

CIVILISATIONS:
THE REPUBLIC OF FOUR-ARMED HUMANOIDS WITH FIVE-FOLD SYMMETRY:
A young, primitive Republic of a single planet, Urbon I, with 5 billion inhabitants.
Major populations:
5 billion Four-Armed Humanoids with Five-fold Symmetry.

PLANETS:
FYNOU VI
A barren planet.

URBON I
A life-bearing planet of the Republic of Four-Armed Humanoids with Five-fold Symmetry.
It is populated by:
5 billion Four-Armed Humanoids with Five-fold Symmetry
Periodical Darkness: Every thirteen years, the orbit of this planet synchronizes with that of a planet closer to its stars, causing a night that lasts for weeks.

FREIA VI
A life-bearing planet.
